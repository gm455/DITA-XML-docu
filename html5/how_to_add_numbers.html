How to add numbers

Here you will find instructions for using the addition function of the JPS Calculation App.

To succussfully add numbers, you must know what addition is.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the add (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.

You want to add 5 to 7.

The equation will look like this: 5 + 7.

You enter 5. Press plus. Enter 7 Press the equal sign.

The result of 12 will appear on the screen.
