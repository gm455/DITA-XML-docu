How to divide numbers

Here you will find instructions for using the multiplication function of the JPS Calculation App.

To successfully divide numbers, you must know what division is

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the divide (รท) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.

The result of the division is displayed.

You want to divide 81 by 9.

The equation will look like this: 81 รท 9.

You enter 81. Press รท. Enter 9. Press the equal sign.

The result of 9 will appear on the screen.
