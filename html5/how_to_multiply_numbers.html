How to multiply numbers

Here you will find instructions for using the multiplication function of the JPS Calculation App.

To succussfully multiply numbers, you must know what multiplication is.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the multiplication (X) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.

The result of the multiplication is displayed.

You want to multiply 9 by 6.

The equation will look like this: 9 X 6.

You enter 9. Press X. Enter 6 Press the equal sign.

The result of 54 will appear on the screen.
